2016 Olympic champion weightlifter disqualified for doping
Olympic weightlifting champion Nijat Rahimov was strippped of his 2016 gold medal and banned for eight years for doping on Tuesday. The Court of Arbitration for Sport said the Kazakh lifter was guify of "four urine substitutions" and disqualified from all his results since March 2016. Rahimov's world record at the 2016 Rio de Janeiro Olympics was controversial even at the time. It came one year after he served a previous ban for doping while competing for Azerbaijian.

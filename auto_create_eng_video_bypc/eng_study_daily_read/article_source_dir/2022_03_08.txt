Companies with female leaders outperform those dominated by men
Women should play a central role in the UK's post-pandemic economic recovery, with evidence revealing companies with more female leaders outperform those dominated by men. According to House of Commons research, accusing the government of ignoring women's needs during the coronavirus pandemic and side-lining them in plans for recovery, the shadow secretary for women and equalities. Anneliese Dodd, said the data showed women held the key to a stronger economy.

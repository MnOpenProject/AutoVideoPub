Live from space, Shenzhou-13 taikonauts deliver 2nd Tiangong class
The Shenzhou-13 taikonauts delivered a second live session of the Tiangong classroom lecture series from China's Tianhe space station core module some 400 kilometers above Earth on Wendesday, and this time students from Xinjiang Uygur and Xizang autonomous regions joined the space-Earth interaction. The three taikonauts in the session interated with students from the Chinese capital Beijing, Urumqi of China's Xinjiang region and Lhasa of China's Xizang region.
